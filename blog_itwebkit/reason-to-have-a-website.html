Why Every Business need a Website?
These questions are answered in this video given below it will clear your all doubts regarding website.
There are some important points about having website which are mentioned in this video.

1. Your customers expect it.
When you have a business with lot of clients then definitely many of clients have asked you about your business website an when you don't have one your reaction must be like "ummm... sorry i don't have one". Sometime these type of questions create embarrassing situations when a well known business doesn't have a website. So, to avoid these situation everyone should have a business website even your business is large or on small scale.

2. It provides social proof.
Website is a social proof for your business it contains your all social links (facebook, twitter, linkedin, youtube and more) where your business history and many things that clients should know are given.

3. Your competitors all have company websites.
In Digital world there is a lot of business earn online and almost all type businesses have competitors. We know if we want to grow more than our competitors we have to do something better then them and having a website is a great initiative. A website explains your business and attract new customers so, what are you waiting for hurry up and get a website before your competitors have one.

4.You’ll shown up in Google search results.
When a business or a company name shows in google search results then it gives great impact to your new customers when they search for products or services which your business have. Google results always helps you to get more customers and there is no need to tell how it will ? .




5. It showcase your products and services.
Whenever a business add new products or service in their listing they have to update their old customers about it and they also need to promote new products to get more customers via social media, online ads, tv ads etc. but when you have a website there is only need to update your product listing on your website.

6. It’s all your information in one place.
A website contain about section which tell viewers about what is your business about what is your dealing criteria and how you deliver your services and goods. Here you can also mention about your company's history your team/staff and carrier opportunities.

7. Tell customer who and where you are.
Your website help to reach your customer to you by providing contact and contacting person's info. you can also add a map to your website's page to gave proper direction of your office address.

8. It attract new customers.
From these seven points its clear that a website help to gain more customers and how a website helps to give a boost on growth of your business.

So, have a business website to boost your business growth and join the revolution of this digital world.
